About us

Researh and Innovation Center for startups & Enterpreneurs (RAISE) was established wih the help of State Government of Uttar Pradesh on 2021. RAISE is a social incubation of Rajkiya Engineering College Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh, that aims to nurture start-ups working on technolgy proving an enabling environment for growth of venture. The incubator seeks to promate Students/Enterpreneurs by proving them righit support system such as mentoring & guiding support.

Mission

To provide best support system for start-ups within our, incubation Centre.

Vision

To Build a vibrant start-up ecosystem by promoting new technology / Knowledge / innovation based Startups.
